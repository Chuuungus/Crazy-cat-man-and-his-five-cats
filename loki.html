2. Loki

"Hungry Chunk"

The second oldest and ex-stray. Out of the five, she is the only one with real experience surviving in the wild. After landing a more comfortable life, she has developed a more laid-back approach in life and now entered the circular dimension. She has now become "CHUNKY" as the internet would describe it. She is the only one with a chipped ear and is the widest out of the five. She is the Mum of the group and can only be motivated with food.
